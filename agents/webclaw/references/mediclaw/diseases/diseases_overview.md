# Diseases - Overview (General Medicine)

## What is General Medicine?
General Medicine (Internal Medicine) is the medical specialty dealing with the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of adult diseases. It encompasses a broad range of conditions affecting all organ systems.

## Major Disease Categories
| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| Cardiovascular | Coronary artery disease, heart failure, arrhythmias, hypertension |
| Respiratory | COPD, asthma, pneumonia, interstitial lung disease |
| Gastrointestinal | GERD, peptic ulcer disease, IBD, cirrhosis |
| Renal | Acute kidney injury, chronic kidney disease, glomerulonephritis |
| Endocrine | Diabetes mellitus, thyroid disorders, adrenal disorders |
| Neurologic | Stroke, epilepsy, Parkinson's disease, multiple sclerosis |
| Infectious | Bacterial, viral, fungal, parasitic infections |
| Hematologic | Anemia, coagulation disorders, leukemia, lymphoma |
| Oncologic | Solid tumors, hematologic malignancies |
| Rheumatologic | Rheumatoid arthritis, SLE, gout, vasculitis |
| Psychiatric | Depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, schizophrenia |
