# Texas Supreme Court

## Court Information
- **Court Name**: Supreme Court of Texas
- **Website**: https://www.txcourts.gov/supreme
- **Phone**: (512) 463-1312
- **Fax**: (512) 463-1365
- **Address**: 201 W 14th Street, Austin, TX 78701
- **Clerk Email**: supremecourtclerk@txcourts.gov
- **Chief Justice**: Jimmy Blacklock
- **Number of Justices**: 9
- **Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM CT

## Jurisdiction
- Final appellate jurisdiction for civil cases
- Constitutional questions
- Original jurisdiction in certain matters
- Disciplinary actions against attorneys
- Administrative supervision over all Texas courts

## Note: Texas has a bifurcated system:
- Supreme Court handles civil appeals
- Court of Criminal Appeals handles criminal appeals
